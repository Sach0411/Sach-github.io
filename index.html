Who I Am

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at KIET Deemed to be University, Ghaziabad, with a deep interest in design, simulation, and making things that actually work in the real world.

My experience spans the full design-to-manufacture pipeline — from modelling complex assemblies in SolidWorks, running ANSYS structural FEA on critical components, all the way to coordinating with machinists and running FDM prototypes.

Beyond mechanical work, I build IoT systems and Python automation tools — because the best engineers understand both the physical and digital layers of a product.
